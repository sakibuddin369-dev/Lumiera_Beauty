About Lumeira Beauty

Thanaka is a traditional cosmetic paste made from grinding the bark of the Thanaka tree (native to Myanmar). It has been used for centuries as a natural skincare remedy. Thanaka not only cools the skin but also prevents acne, promotes smoothness, and provides sun protection. We bring you authentic, organic Thanaka products straight from Myanmar.
